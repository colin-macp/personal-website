C02 Emissions Time Series Modeling

This project was the final project for Econ 104: Data Science for Economists at UCLA. The project required us to fit a time series model and a panel data model. For the time series model, we chose to investigate whether C02 emissions and Energy usage could be used to forecast Global Temperature changes. For the panel data model, we explored the differences in using pooled, fixed effects, and random effects to model different country's life expectancies using predictors such as GDP, Years of Schooling, and Expenditure on health care.

Software: R, RStudio
